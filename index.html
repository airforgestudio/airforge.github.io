Every feature in AirForge exists because radio production teams need it. Nothing more, nothing that gets in the way.

Multitrack Producer

Up to 16 tracks. Drag, arrange, trim, split, and fade clips. Build carts, beds, VO stacks, stingers, and promos fast.

Waveform Editor

Stereo L/R display with sample-accurate selection. Cut, copy, paste, silence, and fade with full undo history.

SCOT / WideOrbit Metadata

Asset ID, title, artist, outcue, dates, Intro and EOM markers — all embedded in the WAV file on export.

Batch Processing

Convert, normalize, rename, tag, and process entire folders of files. Consistent standards across your whole library in one pass.

Effects Suite

Phone Filter, AM Radio, VO Brightener, HPF/LPF, Delay, Room, Hall, and Reverb — live in the Producer or applied in the Editor.

Smooth, Reliable Playback

The Phoenix engine keeps up with fast radio workflows — stutter-free across multiple tracks with effects, instant transport response.

Two workspaces, one workflow

One app. Two ways to work.

Switch between workspaces without losing your place. Markers, metadata, and session state follow you everywhere.

Producer

Build and arrange

Multitrack timeline for fast drag-and-drop radio production. Build carts, beds, stacks, stingers, and promos.

  • Up to 16 tracks — add and remove as needed
  • Drag, trim, split, copy, paste, and fade clips
  • Per-track mute, solo, volume, and FX chain
  • Intro/EOM marker flags directly on the ruler
  • Export directly from the session
Editor

Edit with precision

Stereo L/R waveform view for detailed editing, marker placement, metadata entry, and broadcast-spec export.

  • Sample-accurate selection, L and R channels
  • Cut, copy, paste, silence, and fade
  • Intro/EOM markers matching the Producer style
  • SCOT metadata directly in the Editor panel
  • Save, Save As, and WideOrbit export

Simple workflow

From raw audio to broadcast cart
in four steps.

01

Drop your files

Drag WAV or MP3 files onto any track. AirForge handles conforming automatically — no format juggling.

02

Arrange and edit

Build your production in the Producer or open clips in the Editor for detailed waveform work.

03

Set markers and metadata

Place Intro and EOM cues. Fill in SCOT metadata. Audition the full mix before committing.

04

Export broadcast-ready

One click exports a WideOrbit-ready WAV with all markers and metadata embedded. Done.
